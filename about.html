About QuickCart

QuickCart is an Indian online grocery platform built to fit the rhythm of busy urban life. Instead of visiting multiple stores for fruits, vegetables, staples and snacks, customers can place a single order and receive everything at their doorstep in minutes.

Behind every delivery is a network of local markets, farmer partnerships and brand tie-ups. Our operations team focuses on freshness, safe handling and transparent pricing so that families can confidently rely on QuickCart for their weekly and monthly grocery planning.

With each new locality we add, our goal remains simple: make access to quality groceries as easy as unlocking your phone.
Founded 2025
Operating cities Delhi NCR, Bengaluru, Mumbai, Pune
Average delivery time Under 25 minutes on eligible orders
